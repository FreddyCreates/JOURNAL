🔗 Relationship to THESIS

THESIS is the research verification engine — it reasons about claims, evidence, and proof posture. CIVOS-PRIME sits above THESIS as the constitutional layer. Where THESIS asks "Is this claim verified?", CIVOS-PRIME asks "Are all agents telling the same truth?"

CIVOS-PRIME ensures that THESIS verification results align with AURO declarations, ORIGO implementations, and CODEX executions. It is the system that prevents the organism from contradicting itself — the coherence guarantee of the entire architecture.
